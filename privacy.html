Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how QuietLabs Studio ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the Snapt Chrome extension ("Extension").

1. No Data Collection

Snapt does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or user information. The Extension operates entirely within your browser using Chrome's local storage APIs. No data ever leaves your device.

2. Data Stored Locally

All session data (saved windows, tabs, tab group names, and related settings) is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage via the chrome.storage.local API. This data is never transmitted to any server and is only accessible by the Extension on your device.

3. License Key Verification

License key validation is performed entirely on-device using a cryptographic algorithm. No network request is made to verify your license. Your license key is stored locally and is never sent to any server.

4. No Analytics or Tracking

Snapt does not include any analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind. We have no visibility into how you use the Extension.

5. No Third-Party Services

Snapt does not integrate with or load any third-party scripts, SDKs, or services within the Extension itself.

6. Payments

Purchases are processed by Paddle, our merchant of record. Payment and billing information is collected and handled by Paddle under their own privacy policy. We do not receive or store your payment details.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ghostinthemachine.labs@proton.me.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
